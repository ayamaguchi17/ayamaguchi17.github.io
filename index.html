About me

I began my career in 2001 when the popularity of animated flash websites brought dynamic experience to web users. I was fascinated by how moving objects could create such a difference in the web surfing experience. My role as a multimedia designer allowed me to serve clients from a wide range of industries. There, I honed my skills in various areas of creative production including graphic design, web design, 2D and 3D production.

I became a Creative Director in 2005 and provided multi-disciplinary creative solutions to clients from all over the world. Technology and design evolve rapidly over time and I am constantly updating my skills in order to stay ahead of trend. 3D design, motion graphics and video production are essential tools that I utilize in my current projects.
