About me

I began my career as a web designer in 2001 when animated flash websites brought dynamic experience to web users. I was fascinated by how moving objects could create such a difference in the web surfing experience. I became a multimedia designer in a production studio and had the opportunity to serve clients from a wide range of industries. There, I honed my skills in the creative production of graphic, web design, 2D and 3D animation.

Years of real world experience provided the path to advance my career and I became a Creative Director providing multi-disciplinary creative solutions to multinational clients. Technology and design evolve rapidly over time and I am constantly updating my skills in order to stay ahead of the trend. UX/UI design, coding skills, video editing and motion graphics are essential tools that I use today in my projects.
