About me

I started my career as a designer in 2001 when animated flash websites brought dynamic experience to web visitors. I was fascinated by how unconventional layouts and moving objects could create such a difference in the web surfing experience. I became a multimedia designer in a production studio and had the opportunity to serve clients from a wide range of industries. There, I honed my skills in creative production making use of graphic and web design, online and offline 2D animation, 3D animation, and multimedia in my projects.


After a few years of practical experience, I progressed my career and I became a Creative Director providing multi-disciplinary creative solutions to multinational clients. Technology and design evolve quickly over time and I am constantly updating my skills in order to stay ahead of the trend. UX/UI design, coding, video editing and motion graphics became essential tools that I utilize in delivering multi-platform promotional tools for my clients.
